## 5. How a Workflow Actually Works

Now that you've seen what's possible, let's understand how it all works under the hood.

[SCREENSHOT: Annotated workflow diagram showing the three phases - input nodes (green), processing nodes (blue), and output nodes (orange) with arrows indicating data flow]

A **workflow** is your complete GenAI logic saved as a reusable recipe:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start nodes">
    Load the model & prompt
  </Step>
  <Step title="Middle nodes">
    Do the mixing & cooking—*samplers*, *upscalers*, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="End nodes">
    Plate the meal—save or show the image
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you love buzzwords: yes, it's a **Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG)**, but you can ignore that for now.
</Note>

ComfyUI highlights valid connections and stops you from wiring things that don't match—like child-proof sockets for beginners.

[SCREENSHOT: ComfyUI editor showing invalid connection attempt with red warning indicator and valid connection with green highlight]

## 6. Key Parts You'll Hear About

| Term | Think of it as... | Why it matters |
|------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Node** | A single LEGO® brick | Does one job (e.g., "Load model") |
| **Workflow** | The finished LEGO® set | Runs the whole image pipeline. Saved as `.json` |
| **Model / Checkpoint** | A cookbook | Holds all the AI knowledge—whether for images, text, video, or audio |
| **Custom Node** | A fan-made LEGO® piece | Community add-ons—try new ideas without waiting for official updates |
| **Pod (FlowScale)** | A line cook in the cloud | Executes your workflow on a rented GPU |

## 7. Try It Yourself — Your First Workflow

Ready to see the magic in action? Here's a simple workflow to get you started:

[SCREENSHOT: Step-by-step visual guide showing each of the 5 steps being performed in the ComfyUI interface, from loading a model to saving the final output]

<Steps>
  <Step title="Load Model">
    Choose your AI model (SDXL for images, LLaMA for text, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Input">
    Type your prompt: "A dreamy sunset over Jaipur's Hawa Mahal" or any creative idea
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Processing">
    Set parameters like steps (30), strength (7), or sampling method
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process Output">
    Convert the AI output to your desired format (image, text, video)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Results">
    Your creation lands in the `/outputs` folder, ready to share
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Run it once—you've created your first GenAI workflow! Whether it's art, text, or any other AI-generated content, you're now part of the no-code AI revolution.
</Tip>

## 8. Tiny Glossary (Bookmark This!)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Prompt">
    Text that describes the image you want
